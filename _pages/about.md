Halo Mind is an independent research group specializing in efficient deep learning, sequence modeling, space-time architectures, NLP, Computer Vision and algorithmic robustness. Operating at the intersection of continuous physical dynamics and discrete neural computation, we engineer hardware-efficient neural mechanisms designed for high-throughput, volatile data streams and much more..

Our primary architectural focus encompasses sub-quadratic **State Space Models (Mamba)** and **Deep Delta Learning (DDL)**. We design self-contained, uncertainty-aware frameworks capable of autonomous anomaly rejection, real-time distribution shift adaptation, and trustworthy medical image analysis and much more..

Operating outside traditional, heavily funded academic pipelines, our methodology is driven by absolute resource constraint. This environment forces rigorous architectural innovation, exhaustive ablation, and mathematically bounded efficiency over brute-force computation.
